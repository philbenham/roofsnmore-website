Common questions

Window questions we hear all the time.

How much will new windows actually save me?

Replacing original single-pane aluminum windows (pre-2000) typically cuts cooling costs 15–25%. At resale you'll see 67–72% ROI. In coastal zones, impact-rated windows can save $500–$1,500 on insurance premiums too.

What technologies should I look for?

For Houston, the big four are: Low-E glass coating (reflects heat), argon gas fill (insulates the gap), impact-resistant options (coastal wind zones), and noise reduction glazing. We'll explain which combination makes sense for your home and budget.

What window styles do you install?

We install double-hung, sliding, casement, and picture/bay windows. Double-hung is the most popular in Houston — easy to clean, works with any home style, and holds up well to Gulf Coast humidity.

Do I qualify for the 30% federal tax credit?

If your new windows are ENERGY STAR certified — which all of ours are — you qualify for a 30% federal tax credit up to $600 per year. We provide all the NFRC labels and manufacturer certification paperwork you need at tax time.
