Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need to give you an estimate and do the work. We don't sell your info. We don't spam you. Here's the long version.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Who we are

Roofs-N-More LLC ("we," "us") operates this website and provides roofing, siding, window, and gutter services in the greater Houston, TX area. Our office is at 437 Holly Branch Ln, Kemah, TX 77565. You can reach us at 832-991-0928 or hello@roofs-n-more.com.

What we collect

  • Contact information you give us voluntarily — name, email, phone, address, zip code — when you request an estimate, book a call, or message us.
  • Photos or documents you upload — roof photos, insurance paperwork, previous bids — used only to prepare your quote.
  • Automatic information — IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL. Used to understand site traffic and fix bugs.
  • Cookies — a small file your browser stores. We use cookies for basic site function and Google Analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

How we use it

  1. To prepare and deliver your estimate
  2. To schedule and coordinate your service
  3. To follow up on warranty questions after the job
  4. To respond to messages you send us
  5. To occasionally send relevant service reminders (seasonal maintenance, warranty notices) — you can opt out of these any time

What we don't do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your information. Ever.
  • We don't share your info with advertising networks, lead aggregators, or other roofers.
  • We don't call you repeatedly. One follow-up after an estimate, then we leave you alone.

Who we share it with

Only the people we absolutely need to, to do the work:

  • Our suppliers and material vendors when ordering your specific materials
  • Your insurance company (with your permission) for storm-damage claims
  • Payment processors (Stripe, Square) when you pay by card
  • Legal authorities if required by subpoena or court order

Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) for this site and store customer records in access-controlled software. We're a small team — only Dale, Rosa, and two office staff ever see your file. We keep records for 10 years to honor our warranty, then we delete them.

Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Request a copy of the information we have on you
  • Ask us to correct or update it
  • Ask us to delete it (unless we need to keep it for an active warranty)
  • Opt out of all non-transactional communications

Email hello@roofs-n-more.com or call us at 832-991-0928 and Rosa will handle it within 7 business days.

Third-party links

Our site links to manufacturer websites (Owens Corning, James Hardie, Pella, etc.) and social media. We don't control their privacy practices — read their policies separately.

Updates to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll post the new version here and update the date at the top. We won't email you every minor change.

Questions?

Call us at 832-991-0928 or email hello@roofs-n-more.com. Real human reply, usually same day.
