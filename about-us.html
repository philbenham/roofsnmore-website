A note about Texas

Texas doesn't actually license roofers.

That's not a small detail. It means anyone with a ladder and a pickup truck can legally call themselves a roofer in this state. Which is exactly how Houston homeowners end up burned.

So before you hire any roofer in Texas — including us — ask three questions: Who's insured for the work? Who pulls the permits? And whose name is on the warranty? Roofs-N-More carries insurance on every job, pulls every permit, and the warranty is signed by the owner. That's the bar. Anyone working below it shouldn't be on your roof.
