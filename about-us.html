About Roofs-N-More

Houston's trusted home exterior crew — founded in Kemah, built on honesty.

We started in 2015 to give Houston homeowners a contractor they could actually trust. A decade later, we've installed 850+ roofs and hundreds of exterior projects — and we still answer the phone ourselves.

Our story

Started on a ladder in Kemah. Still climbing it.

Roofs-N-More was founded in 2015 by local tradesmen right here in Kemah, TX. We got started because we kept watching out-of-town contractors show up after storms — take deposits, do shoddy work, and disappear before the next rain. We knew we could do better.

A decade later: 850+ roofs installed, hundreds of homes sided, thousands of windows replaced. We're still a crew you can call. No call centers, no high-pressure sales, no "limited-time" scare tactics. Just a small team of certified pros who give you a straight answer, a fair price, and work that holds up.

— Phil & the crew
